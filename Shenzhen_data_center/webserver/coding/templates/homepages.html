What is Individualized Pair Analysis of Gene Expression(IPAGE):

(1) The gene expression data including two groups of samples.

(2) Transfer gene expression matrix to gene pair matrix. Relative expression between every gene pairs were calculated in each sample.

(3) Statistical analysis across all samples. Gene pairs with Fisher’s exact test P-value<10−16 were kept for subsequent analysis.

(4) Schematic of the expression of the identified gene pairs across the two groups of samples.

(B) Selecting reverse gene pairs using biological pathways.
